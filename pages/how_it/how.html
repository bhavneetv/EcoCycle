How It Works

Timeline for Recycling

Timeline for Recycling

1st Step

Scan Barcode

The user scans the barcode of the plastic or can bottle using the website’s scanner. The barcode is checked against a database to verify the item type.

2nd Step

Point Allocation

If the item is identified as plastic, the user earns 2 points. For cans, 5 points are awarded. The points are automatically added to the user’s score.

3rd Step

Accumulate Points

The user continues to recycle and accumulate points. Once their total reaches 20 points or more, they become eligible to request a pickup.

4th Step

Request Pickup

When the user’s points exceed 20, they can click a button to request a nearby recycler to collect their bottles. The recycler arrives and verifies the items.

5th Step

Redeem Rewards

After verification, the user is awarded Amazon gift card points based on the number and type of items recycled, rewarding them for their eco-friendly efforts.
